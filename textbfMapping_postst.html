Mapping post-stroke white matter disconnection with HARDI diffusion MRI tractography. We have been conducting HARDI tractography studies, focusing on the effects of disconnection of major white matter tracts on cognition and language. The technique we are using, streamline tractography with constrained spherical deconvolution (CSD) [32, 33], allows for the resolution of crossing-fibers (multiple tracts passing through the same region) and provides high resolution reconstructions of white matter anatomy (Figure 11).
